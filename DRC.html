This is how generous single rooms can be – and how relaxing: beds that promise a good night’s sleep, warm colours and stylish details that ensure a comfortable atmosphere. Our classic single rooms give you a comfortable refuge on your visit to Munich

Kept in the traditional country house style, you immediately feel completely at home in our “classics”. Light colours and high quality wood elements contribute to a friendly atmosphere. With every night you stay, you get a little slice of Bavaria.
