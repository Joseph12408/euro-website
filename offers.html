CITY BREAKS

Munich is always a good place to go, whether you´re visiting for a long weekend or for longer. Munich is home to wonderful attractions; it´s known for both its seasonal festivals and a rich cultural calendar all year round. It’s a pleasure for us to help plan memorable Munich city breaks for our guests during their stay at our hotel. Get to know the best sides of the city for yourself with an exciting short trip that promises not to disappoint. Bavaria´s capital offers beautiful sights, parks, churches, and museums. For instance, Munich´s Kunstareal offers a museum neighbourhood across a small, highly walkable area in the city center and in the vibrant Maxvorstadt district. Visitors can dive into 5,000 years of history with countless architectural and cultural gems to admire. Make sure not to miss this district during your Munich city break and discover Europe´s most important cultural hubs. From the splendid Marienplatz, to the special Viktualienmarkt, to countless famous beer gardens, you can spend a wonderful time exploring the unique spots Munich has to offer. To make the most of your Munich city break you should see the English Garden, the BMW Museum, the Rathaus (Townhall) and the Nymphenburg Baroque Palace. If you want to feel like a local, don’t forget to taste the Bavarian beer and food. Delicious! Located just a few steps away from Hotel Excelsior is Munich´s Botanical Garden in the suburb of Nymphenburg – it is one of the best and famous botanical gardens in the world. This is the perfect place to enjoy your Munich city break close to nature with more than 19,500 plants to see. At Hotel Excelsior by Geisel, we have created some offers for your Munich city breaks, so that you can concentrate on the most important thing: your wellbeing! On request, our Hotel will be pleased to create a special arrangement according to your wishes.
