WINE-HOTEL

The Latin term excelsior means something like “the excellent” and that describes our first-class hotel in Munich city center perfectly. Highest standard of hotel and excellent service meet with Bavarian hospitality. The location is also excellent: the central train station can be reached within just a five minutes' walk by foot, and the famous Karlsplatz is just a few steps away. As you will see, whether it’s for shopping or a city tour, the Hotel Excelsior is the ideal starting point for your stay in Munich.

ROOMS & SUITS

Stylish yet cozy – traditional Bavarian charm meets modern amenities at the Hotel Excelsior. 114 high-class hotel rooms and suites welcome you with an exclusive yet friendly atmosphere right in the heart of Munich. The Hotel Excelsior by Geisel is one of the most renowned hotels in Munich city. The hotel rooms not only offer the highest level of comfort and modern facilities, the size of the rooms also exceeds the usual standard. Bright colors, elegant design and lots of natural light ensure your feeling of well-being.
